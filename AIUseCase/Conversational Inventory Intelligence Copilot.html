Executive Summary

This Generative AI-powered Inventory Copilot enables natural language access to inventory data, helping supply chain teams identify risks, optimize inventory levels, improve service, and make faster, data-driven decisions.

Strategic Vision & Field Imperative

Resolving severe field blind spots without increasing reporting framework bloat across Aerospace, Manufacturing, Distribution, and Retail industries.

Business Challenges

  • Visibility Bottlenecks: Fragmented metrics across regional networks delay proactive supply chain planning strategy execution.
  • Operational Exposure: Rising stockout windows and accumulating obsolete inventory assets drain working capital.
  • Friction Points: Critical operational decisions remain locked behind technical developer query and request backlogs.
