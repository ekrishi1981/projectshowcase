Executive Brief

This automated multi-modal system converts raw screen layouts into actionable natural language decision summaries, removing complexity from static executive dashboard interactions.

Strategic Shift & Vision

Shifting BI telemetry boundaries from static, passive data presentation to automated, proactive conversational decision intelligence models.

Business Challenges

  • Dashboard Overload: Floods of fragmented metrics across regional visual portals create severe executive cognitive overload.
  • Interpretation Bottlenecks: Lack of automated, real-time context delays active business outlier parsing and root cause extraction.
  • Developer Dependency: Critical business context loops are locked behind manual analyst workflows and developer ticket backlogs.
